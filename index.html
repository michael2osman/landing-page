The Cat Clinic

Welcome to your cat's newest favorite hospital. Does your cat hate going to your local vet? Does she meow or even hiss when you let her out of that carrier just to see a scary human doctor? Take your cat to The Cat Clinic, the first and only veterinary practicion for cats run by cats!

Meet Our Doctors!

Miso

-Head Technician

Our founder and head doctor, Miso! Miso is an orange british shorthair and scottish shorthair mix. She received her Bachelor's at Cat State University and did her Master's and Residecy at The University of Cats. Miso is our head doctor and specializes in aggression control. She loves exercising and making biscuits on her free time!

Chai

-Nutritionist

This is Chai, our first hired employee and specialist in cat nutrition. Chai is a persian who received her full education at the University of Felines. Chai loves to take cat naps with her humans on her free time, and her kindness and patience will have all of your cats loving her medical expertise!

Mochi

-Personal Trainer

Mochi is the latest addition to The Cat Clinic. Mochi is a European shorthair mix whose origins are unknown; she was previously homeless but decided to go to school to become a doctor, where she received her degrees specializing in Cat training and exercise. Mochi loves head pets and running down random streets chasing cars on her free time. Mochi will have your lazy cat up and active in no time!
